About Savergo

Even if you love everything about your job, going through the same old routine 250 days a year is no way to live a life.

Our brains, bodies and taste buds thrive on new experiences, spontaneity and serendipity.

That’s why we created Savergo.

Our vision is to make the workweek happier, healthier and more fun for employees everywhere.

Here’s how it works

We build relationships (real face-to-face ones) with the most loved restaurants, bars, shops, salons, gyms and entertainment venues in areas where lots of people work.

Then these local partners dream up creative offers exclusively for hard-working locals.

Free dishes at local lunch spots, half-off the best yoga classes, a season-end private shoe sale, a complimentary blow dry, discounts on gym memberships and more.

Our focus is on the best local spots and real offers that we’d jump at in a heartbeat.

3 things make Savergo useful and unique

We're “hyper-local”, we're exclusively for employees and we only allow quality offers on the platform.

These offers pop up each day (via email or mobile) and open the eyes (and mouths) of local employees to new experiences and offers that help save lots of hard-earned money on everyday purchases.

For local merchants, your success is our success

We care about how enjoyable and effective we are as a partner in doing one simple thing: helping businesses and their employees find and enjoy what you bring to the local community.

This App

The Savergo App is a free app that allows Savergo members (employees) to access the Savergo platform.

Employees have access to exclusive deals offered by our local merchant partners. Savergo is currently available in the Soho and Shoreditch areas of London.

Employees need to exchange Savergo credits for deal tickets. Many types of deals and benefits are offered on Savergo, but they are typically limited in number and available for only a short time.

Savergo credits are not free. Employers can subscribe to our service and offer the benefits to their entire workforce.

Merchants interested in partnering with us can contact us, info@savergo.com or see our website for further details.

Savergo Limited founded 2012 in London, UK.
